Who is Voorhees?


Ever since I hit 13, my entire world has revolved around one incredible thing: Art.

Around the age of 14, I stumbled into the world of music.
Armed with a laptop and a heap of determination,
I dove headfirst into recording, producing, and all the technical wizardry that comes with it, right from the cozy confines of my bedroom in Varna, Bulgaria.

In the beginning, I was a bit of a bashful artist, hesitating to share my musical creations with anyone.
Yet, as days turned into months, I started building up the courage to play my tracks for a few of my pals.
Their genuine enthusiasm and support gave me the push I needed to step out of my comfort zone and share my music more widely.

Then, the thought struck me: Why not put my art out there for the world to see?
And that's where "Voorhees" comes in.
At first i struggled finding an alias that i really liked and after going through some horible names i had an idea.
You see, I'm not just a music and art enthusiastâ€”I'm also a horror film fanatic.
And if we're talking horror icons, there is none better than Jason Voorhees.
I liked how it sounded and people seemed to like it so it stuck.

So how does digital art comes into this?
You see, I needed someone to do my covers, videos and visuals.
At first, it was all about necessity. With no go-to artist in sight i decided to give it a try. Over time, it actually became something i enjoyed and i started doing stuff outside of "Voorhees" as well.

Anyway, i hope you enjoy my stuff!
